Alcohol is consumed all over the world and roughly 2 billion people drink alcohol in the world. Drinking alcohol is not unusual as it is drank in many situations by many different types of people. A lot of people use it in social situations where they consume small amounts of alcohol. Alcohol is OK to have every once and a while in small quantities but is not good to overdose on as there are many health risks.

Health risks

  • Liver problems
  • Reduced fertility
  • High blood pressure
  • Increased risk of various cancers
  • Increased risk of a heart attack
  • Raised blood pressure which increases the risk of Stroke
  • stomach disorders
  • depression
  • emotional disorders
