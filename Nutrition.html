Nutrition is a important part of staying healthy. You need to make sure you have a balanced diet consisting of many different food types with similar levels of consumption. Fruit and vegetables should take up a third of your diet. They protect against cancer, obesity and a variety of chronic diseases. Carbohydrates are found in bread, rice, potatoes and pasta theses are a great source of energy.

There are so many different food types and they are all in the eat well plate Look below to see whats in the eat well plate.

  • Fruit and vegetables
  • Meat, fish, eggs, beans
  • Food and drinks high in fat and/or sugar
  • Milk dairy foods
  • Bread, rice, potatoes, pasta
